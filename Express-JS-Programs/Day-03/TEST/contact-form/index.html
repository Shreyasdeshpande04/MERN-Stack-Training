चला जिवाची मुंबई करूया, सागराच्या लाटांवर मनसोक्त डोलूया.  तुमचं साहस इथेच वाट पाहे, मुंबई उभी स्वागताला आहे.

Mumbai, the financial capital of India, is a city that never sleeps. Known for its iconic skyline, colonial architecture, bustling local trains, and the heart of the Bollywood film industry, Mumbai offers a thrilling mix of tradition and modernity.

Whether you're a history lover, a foodie, a beachgoer, or a culture explorer, Mumbai has something special for you. Start your journey at the Gateway of India, take a ferry to the Elephanta Caves, stroll down Marine Drive at sunset, or explore the vibrant streets of Colaba and Bandra.

Top Experiences in Mumbai:

  • Visit Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Take a film city tour and get behind the scenes of Bollywood
  • Shop and snack in Colaba Causeway and Crawford Market
  • Relax at Juhu Beach and Girgaum Chowpatty
  • Explore art at Jehangir Art Gallery and Kala Ghoda

How to book spot on our website?

  • Click on Destination tab on the navbar
  • You will see a dropdown of various types of Destinations like ADVENTURE, HISTORIC, PILGRIM, WILDLIFE
  • Click on the type of destination you want to visit
  • You will see 4 spots of each destination type
  • You can click on any spot to view name, info, photo and book button
  • When you click the book button, a form will appear to enter source, passengers, etc.
  • After submitting the form, you’ll see a success message.
